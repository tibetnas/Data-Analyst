Introduction¶

In this project i will analyze a dataset that contains information of over 10,000 movies. I will check the data, clean the data and finally use the data to answer the below questions.

These are the questions i will be answering:

  • 1. What actor has been in most movies

  • 1.1. List top 10 actors with most appearances in movies?

  • 2. How has movie genres changed over time (list of top 10)

  • 2.1. (Bonus) What are the number/percent of movies created in each genre?
    • </a>

  • 2.2. What movie genres are most profitable over time?
    • </a>

  • 2.3. What movie genres are most profitable over time adjusted for inflation?

    Conclusions¶

    Some limitation to the results in my investigation is that missing values in some categories caused me to drop some data and investigate 10,727 movies instead of 10,866 movies. I also replaced some '0' values with the average and removed one duplicate entry. This is what i concluded from answering the questions: Robert De Niro has been in the most movies. I listed the number of genres produced over time as well as the percentages. I found that adventure movies are most profitable over time based on both average revenue and average revenue adjusted for inflation. Adventure movies are also the most popular movie genre over time.
